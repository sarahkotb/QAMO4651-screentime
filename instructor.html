    Step 4 Law of Large Numbers

    As n grows, the sampling distribution of Ȳ narrows around μ — because Var[Ȳ] = σ²⁄n → 0. We run a round of samples for each n (same x-axis), then check SD[Ȳ] against σ⁄√n.
